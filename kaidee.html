Kaidee

I joined Kaidee in May 2019 as a UX intern and helped the customer design team develop and improve the new Android platform. This was a 4-month project where I learned the ropes of UX Design with the help of my mentors.

Kaidee is Thailand’s biggest online marketplace and serves as a buy-and-sell platform for firsthand and secondhand items.

The Problem
We noticed that there was a drop off in users during the posting flow interaction. Users will enter the app with the intention to sell items but exits halfway without completing their post. Why are users dropping off before finishing their posting?

Our Goal
To identify usability issues during posting and determine key features that will facilitate this process.

Our Process

The user-centered design process that was used for this project:

Step 1: User Research

Research Methods
We spent a week with our primary users to get a detailed view of their process through in-person interviews and online surveys. Some of the questions we asked were…

  1. What are your frustrations with the current app version?
  2. At what stage do you exit the app?
  3. Can you walk us through your latest posting?
  4. Did you complete the process? Why or why not?

For the purpose of our two research interests, we believed that a user survey will help us gather data on general reading habits and objectives while a user interview will give us a deeper understanding of user interaction and thinking process.

Step 5: User Testing

Prototype Evaluation
We interviewed six primary users from a diverse range of demographics (age, gender, occupation). I started preliminary questions regarding their buying-selling habits and expectations for our mobile app.

Then, I handed over our functional prototype and gave them tasks to walk through with the ‘think aloud’ method:

  1. Can you fill in the item title without typing?
  2. Can you put in the highest price for this item based on other users on Kaidee?
  3. Can you find the appropriate category of this toy figurine?
  4. What do you think is the purpose of this page (preview page)?
