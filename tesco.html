Tesco Online Shopping

The Tesco website redesign was a 3-month project where I helped redesign Tesco online grocery platform to improve the ease of use in finding product items.

Tesco is a multinational groceries and merchandise retailer with an online grocery shopping and delivery service.

The Problem
Finding grocery items online is a challenging and time-consuming experience. Users, who regularly shop in person, find it difficult to understand the way our items are categorized online, especially when they cannot customize their search.

Our Goal
The goal of this project was to redesign the Tesco Online Shopping website with a focus on developing a new interaction that will facilitate grocery item search.

ROLE
User Research, Information Architecture, Competitive Analysis, Interaction Design, Prototyping

TIMELINE
Dec 2019 - Feb 2020

PLATFORM
Responsive Web (Desktop)

Step 3: Information Architecture

After brainstorming solutions, we designed a user flow diagram to outline the steps that a user would possibly take—with our new, suggested features—to achieve their goal in ordering an item of interest.


Step 4: Wireframing & Prototyping

Low-Fidelity Wireframes
From our user research and ideation, I created low-fidelity wireframes and a new interaction that addresses our issues. This new interaction focuses on better category organization and treatment of information (discussed in detail in upcoming prototype section).


High Fidelity Prototypes
With success from our low-fidelity wireframes, we began creating our high-fidelity prototypes with an emphasis on the interaction between the different screens to make use of it during user testing.

User Testing
We conducted some quick user testing to evaluate if our users will understand this new flow. We defined three user tasks and asked users to describe their process using the ‘think aloud’ method:

  1. Find the category for ‘eggs’: Success
  2. Search for extra-large, 30-pack, boiled eggs: Success
  3. Identify the source of the eggs on the product page: Success
